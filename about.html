About Us

Our Story and Commitment

DESIGN FAB Aluminum Fabrication, based in Aluva, Eranakulam, is a distinguished leader in the field of aluminum fabrication. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in the comprehensive range of services we offer. Specializing in Aluminium fabrication, Modular kitchen cabinets, Bedroom wardrobes, Toilet doors, Sliding windows, Office partition, and all-encompassing interior works including S S and MS works, we are dedicated to transforming spaces with a perfect blend of functionality and aesthetics.

What sets us apart is our team of highly skilled and trained technicians who bring expertise to every project. At DESIGN Fab, we understand the importance of quality craftsmanship and innovation. Our technicians, available 24/7, are ready to meet all your aluminum fabrication and interior work requirements, ensuring that each project is executed with precision and attention to detail.
