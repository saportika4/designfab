About Us

Our Story

DESIGN FAB Aluminum Fabrication, based in Aluva, Eranakulam, is a distinguished leader in the field of aluminum fabrication. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in the comprehensive range of services we offer. Specializing in Aluminium fabrication, Modular kitchen cabinets, Bedroom wardrobes, Fiber Doors, Sliding windows, Office partition, and all-encompassing interior works including S S and MS works, we are dedicated to transforming spaces with a perfect blend of functionality and aesthetics.
